About Gift Ticket


We're bringing scratch-off lottery to gift-giving!

The receiver is flooded with anticipation when they receive a physical scratch-off card. Unlike lottery scratch-off tickets, this one is rigged to win. The person gifting has fun toying with emotions while being on the receiving-end of enhanced gratitude. It's like a cash gift card without the risk of losing or wasting money!
