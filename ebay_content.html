What's a scratch-off gift card?

This scratch-off is made to look authentic and give an unwitting recipient the thrill and excitement of instant-win lottery.
The best part? It's rigged to win!

How does it work?

  1. Buy card for a 'winning' amount: $25/$50/$100/etc
    All cards cost the same no matter how much you're giving.
  2. We'll ask you to confirm your contact details for redemption
    By default we grab your eBay account details, enter your mobile # to get a text message.
  3. Give or mail card to your recipient
    There's no risk in mailing because you haven't forked over real dough!
  4. Pay your recipient DIRECTLY when they redeem
    We'll notify you with a simple one-click payment link to send money directly to your recipient via PayPal®
